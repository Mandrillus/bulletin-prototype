讃美歌21 3番 「扉を開きて」

Verse 1

Tobira o hirakite ware o michibiki
Makoto no hikari to nagusame miru
Kami no ie e to mukae tamae ya.
扉を開きて　われを導き
まことの光と　慰め満る
神の家へと　迎えたまえや。
Open the door and lead me,
Into truth’s light and perfect comfort,
Receive me into the house of God.

Verse 2

Waga Shu yo, mimae ni ware wa kitarinu.
Warera no kokoro ni Shu mo mata kitari,
Warera o kiyomete, yadori tamae ya.
わが主よ、みまえに　われは来たりぬ。
われらのこころに　主もまた来たり、
われらをきよめて、やどりたまえや。
My Lord, I have come before You.
Come also into our hearts,
Cleanse us, and dwell within us.

Verse 3

Osore ononokite mimae ni kitari,
Kokoro mo karada mo subete o sasagu,
Inori to uta o ukeire tamae.
おそれおののきて　みまえに来たり、
こころもからだも　すべてをささぐ、
祈りと歌を　受け入れたまえ。
In fear and trembling we come before You,
Offering heart and body, our whole being;
Receive our prayers and songs.

Verse 4

Michibiku hoshi naru Shu no mikotoba o
Warera ni ataete tsuneni nagusame,
Yowaki kokoro ni chikara o tamae.
導く星なる　主のみことばを
われらに与えて　つねに慰め、
弱きこころに　力をたまえ。
Give us the Lord’s word, the guiding star,
To comfort us תמיד, always,
And grant strength to our weak hearts.

Verse 5

Katari tamae, Shu yo, inoru ware-ra ni.
Inochi no izumi wa koko ni wakiide,
Kunan no hi ni mo nagusame afuren.
語りたまえ、主よ、祈るわれらに。
いのちのいずみは　ここに湧き出で、
苦難の日にも　慰めあふれん。
Speak, O Lord, to us who pray.
Here springs forth the fountain of life,
And even in days of suffering, comfort will overflow.
