Hymn 21-402 いともとうとき (Itomo tōtoki)

Verse 1

Itomo tōtoki Yesu no megumi
Tsumi ni shinitaru mi o mo ikasu.
Shu yori tamawaru ten no kate ni
Ueshi kokoro mo ima wa michinu
いともとうとき　イエスの恵み
罪に死にたる　身をも活かす。
主よりたまわる　天の糧に
飢えしこころも　いまは満ちぬ。
How precious is the grace of Jesus,
It gives life even to one dead in sin.
By the heavenly bread given from the Lord,
My hungry heart is now filled.

Refrain

Yo ni aru kagiri, Shu nomi sakae to
Itsukushimi to o katari tsutaen
世にあるかぎり、主のみ栄えと
いつくしみとを　語り伝えん
As long as I live in this world, I will proclaim the Lord’s glory,
And His loving mercy.

Verse 2

Sukui no megumi tsuguru ware wa
Yorokobi afure uta to zo naru.
Horobi o idashi kono shirase o,
Amaneku hito ni katari tsutaen
救いの恵み　告ぐるわれは
喜びあふれ　歌とぞなる。
滅びを出し　この知らせを、
あまねく人に　語り伝えん。
I who proclaim the grace of salvation
Overflow with joy and break into song.
Leaving destruction behind, this good news
I will tell to all people everywhere.

Verse 3

Katari tsukusenu Yesu no megumi.
Itomo chiisaki ware o mo meshi,
Ten no yosutsugi to nashitamaba
Tare ka morubeki Shu no sukui ni
語り尽くせぬ　イエスの恵み。
いとも小さき　われをも召し、
天の世嗣と　なしたまえば
たれか洩るべき　主の救いに。
The grace of Jesus cannot be fully told.
He calls even me, so small and unworthy,
And makes me an heir of heaven;
Who then could be left out of the Lord’s salvation?
