讃美歌21 17番 「聖なる主の美しさと」

Verse 1

Seinaru shu no utsukushisa to
Sono sakae o aoi de,
Magokoro mote mi-mae ni tachi
Mi-na o tatae, agameyou.
聖なる主の美しさと
その栄えを　仰いで、
まごころもて　み前に立ち
み名をたたえ、あがめよう。
How beautiful is the holy Lord,
I look upon His glory,
With sincere heart I stand before Him,
And praise and honor His name.

Verse 2

Omomi subete shu ga kawatte
Otte kudasaru ureshisa.
Nayami ooi yo no tabiji mo
Michibiku shu no mi-megumi.
重荷すべて　主が代わって
負ってくださる　うれしさ。
悩み多い　世の旅路も
みちびく　主のみ恵み。
All burdens are taken by the Lord,
What joy that He carries them for us.
Even in the troubled journey of this world,
The Lord’s grace will guide us.

Verse 3

Kokoro tsukushi shu ni tsukaeyou.
Toru ni taranu watashi no
Ai no waza mo, mi mo kokoro mo
Shu wa ukeire, yorokobu.
心つくし　主に仕えよう。
とるに足らぬ　私の
愛のわざも、身も心も
主は受け入れ、よろこぶ。
With all my heart I will serve the Lord,
Even my humble acts of love,
Body and heart alike,
The Lord accepts and rejoices.

Verse 4

Yami o kaete hikari to suru
Shu no megumi no takasa.
Nozomi wa waki, osore wa kie,
Mune ni michiru yorokobi.
闇をかえて　光とする
主の恵みの　貴さ。
望みは湧き、おそれは消え、
胸に満ちる　よろこび。
Turning darkness into light,
Such is the preciousness of the Lord’s grace.
Hope springs up, fear disappears,
And joy fills my heart.
