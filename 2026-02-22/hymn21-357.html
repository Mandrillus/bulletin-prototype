讃美歌21 357番「力に満ちたる」

Verse 1

Chikara ni michitaru shu naru kami wa
Kaze sae mimune no mama ni osamu.
Mikotoba hibikase kataru toki wa
Kagayaku taiyō mo koko ni tomaru.
力に満ちたる　主なる神は
風さえ　み旨のままにおさむ。
み言葉ひびかせ　語るときは
輝く太陽も　ここに止まる。
The Lord God, full of power,
Commands even the wind by His will.
When He speaks and His word resounds,
The shining sun itself stands still.

Verse 2

Sakamaku aranami kishi o uchite
Takamari todoroki semaru toki mo
Chikara ni michitaru shu no miude wa
Tachimachi arashi o shizume tamō.
逆巻く荒波　岸を打ちて
高まりとどろき　迫るときも
力に満ちたる　主のみ腕は
たちまち嵐を　静めたもう。
Though raging waves strike the shore,
Roaring high and pressing near,
The mighty arm of the Lord
At once calms the storm.

Verse 3

Arashi wa takerite yami wa fukaku
Kozue no tori no su uchi tatakedo
Fukaki mikokoro no tsukuri-nushi wa
Miezaru mite mote mamori tamō.
嵐はたけりて　闇は深く
こずえの鳥の巣　打ち叩けど
深きみこころの　つくりぬしは
見えざるみ手もて　守りたもう。
Though storms rage and darkness deepens,
And birds’ nests in the treetops are beaten,
The Creator of deep compassion
Guards with His unseen hand.

Verse 4

Sekai no kuni-guni sono shihai-sha
Mikami no mimune o ononoki kike.
Miwaza o agamete uyamai tsutsu
Mimae ni kitarite mina o homeyō.
世界の国々、その支配者、
み神のみ旨を　おののき聞け。
みわざをあがめて　うやまいつつ
み前に来たりて　み名をほめよ。
Nations of the world and their rulers,
Hear with trembling the will of God.
Exalt His mighty works in reverence,
Come before Him and praise His name.
