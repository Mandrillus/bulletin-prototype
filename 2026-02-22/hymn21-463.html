Hymn 21-463 わが行くみち (Waga yuku michi)

Verse 1

Waga yuku michi itsu ikani
Naru beki ka wa tsuyu shirane do,
Shu wa mi kokoro nashitama wan
わが行くみち　いついかに
なるべきかは　つゆしらねど、
主はみこころ　なしたまわん。
The path I walk, I know not when or how,
Yet the Lord will accomplish His will.

Refrain

Sonae tamou Shu no michi o fumi te yukan, hitosuji ni
そなえたもう　主のみちを
ふみて行かん、　ひとすじに。
I will walk the path the Lord has prepared, steadfastly and true.

Verse 2

Waga kokoro yo tsuyoku are
Hito wa kawari yo wa utsure do,
Shu wa mi kokoro nashitama wan
わが心よ、　強くあれ。
ひとはかわり　世はうつれど
主はみこころ　なしたまわん。
O my heart, be strong.
Though people change and the world shifts,
The Lord will accomplish His will.

Verse 3

Arau mi omo uchi hiraki,
Sunahara ni mo mana o furase,
Shu wa mi kokoro nashitama wan
あらうみおも　うちひらき、
すなはらにも　マナをふらせ、
主はみこころ　なしたまわん。
He opens the rough sea,
And even showers manna on the sand,
The Lord will accomplish His will.
