讃美歌21 459番 「飼い主わが主よ」

Verse 1

Kai-nushi waga Shu yo, mayou ware-ra o
Wakakusa no nobe ni tomonai tamae.
Warera o mamorite yashinai tamae,
Warera wa Shu no mono, Shu no mure nareba.
飼い主わが主よ、まよう我らを
若草の野べに　ともないたまえ。
我らを守りて　養いたまえ、
我らは主のもの、主の群れなれば。
Shepherd, our Lord, we who wander,
Lead us into fields of green.
Guard us and nourish us,
For we are Yours, Your flock.

Verse 2

Yoki tomo to narite tsuneni michibiki,
Mayowaba tazunete tsurekaeri mase.
Warera no inori o ukeire tamae,
Warera wa Shu no mono, tada Shu ni tayoru.
良き友となりて　常にみちびき、
まよわば尋ねて　つれ帰りませ。
我らの祈りを　受け入れたまえ、
我らは主のもの、ただ主に頼る。
Be our good friend and guide us always;
If we stray, seek us and bring us back.
Receive our prayers,
For we are Yours and trust in You alone.

Verse 3

Yurushi no michikai, sukui no megumi,
Kiyomuru chikara wa tada Shu ni zo aru.
Warera o aganai inochi o tamau,
Warera wa Shu no mono, Shu ni arite iku.
赦しのみちかい、救いのめぐみ、
きよむる力は　ただ主にぞある。
我らをあがない　生命をたまう、
我らは主のもの、主に在りて生く。
The covenant of forgiveness, the grace of salvation,
The power to cleanse is found in You alone.
You redeem us and grant us life;
We are Yours, living in You.

Verse 4

Shu yo, itsukushimi o ware-ra ni mitashi,
Ima yori mimune o nasashime tamae.
Warera o awaremu megumi fukashi,
Warera wa Shu no mono, tada Shu o aisu.
主よ、いつくしみを　我らに満たし、
今よりみむねを　なさしめたまえ。
我らをあわれむ　み恵みふかし、
我らは主のもの、ただ主を愛す。
Lord, fill us with Your loving mercy,
And lead us to do Your will from now on.
Your compassion toward us is deep;
We are Yours, and we love You alone.
